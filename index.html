HOW TO MAKE THE BEST MATCHA:

  • 3 grams of matcha power
  • 6 oz of oakmilk (or milk of your choice)
  • Water at 176℉
  • vanilla syrup of your choice

    Tools you need:

  • Bowl
  • Bamboo whisk or milk frother
  • Measuring cup
         
  1. Add ice cubes, oatmilk, and simple syrup to a glass cup
  2. sift the matcha powder into a bowl. This will minimize any lumps and yeild a smooth latte.
  3. add 1 1/2 tablespoons of water to the bowl.
  4. whisk briskly, drawing a letter W, not the usual circular pattern. Blend the hot water and matcha powder until no lumps remain. The matcha should be smooth and a little foamy.
  5. Pour the matcha mixture into the glass cup!
